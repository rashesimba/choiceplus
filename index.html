Who are we?

ChoicePlus Care is founded in May 2019 with the aim of providing affordable, high quality healthcare solutions to both private and public sector. We offer health qualified staff and support workers within a diverse number of settings.

What we do?

We are dedicated to providing the best care possible for our clients, ChoicePlus Care offers a range of healthcare recruitment services to clients that require reliable, cost effective and high quality temporary staffing solutions to meet all needs and budgets. While we work with many of our clients on a contractual basis where we can anticipate staffing requirements, there are always emergencies and our 24 hour service means that we can have the appropiately skilled healthcare professional at any time of the day or night.

Our Guarantee

ChoicePlus Care are committed to achieving the highest standards in the care industry. Our aim is to empower, provide insight and inspire our clients and members alike. We enhance client satisfaction by addressing their needs proactively and effectively by offering an accessible, responsive service to all our clients and members.

We supply to:.

NHS Hospitals and Trusts, Community Packages, Private Hospitals, Local Authority and Private Nursing Homes, Mental Health, Learning Difficulty and Supported living. Our services and specialities include Live in care; Personal Care; Holiday Care; Social and community interaction; Drug and Alcohol; complex needs; Dementia and Mental Health. Our team is highly professional and widely experienced within the current healthcare environment. .
